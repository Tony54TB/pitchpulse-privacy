PitchPulse Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 4, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to PitchPulse. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information that may be collected through your use of our mobile app and website.

2. Data We Collect

Usage Data: We may collect anonymous analytics data regarding app performance and general usage patterns to help improve the PitchPulse experience.

Advertising Data: Our free version includes third-party advertisements (e.g., via AdMob). The advertising ID of your device may be used by the ad provider to serve relevant non-intrusive ads. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

We do NOT collect: Personal identification data (like names, exact locations, emails, or phone numbers) without your explicit consent.

3. How We Use It

Any collected data is used exclusively to maintain our services, provide relevant live score updates, ensure the AI match companion functions smoothly, and display non-intrusive advertisements.

4. Children's Privacy

PitchPulse does not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13. If you think that your child provided this kind of information on our App, we strongly encourage you to contact us immediately and we will do our best efforts to promptly remove such information from our records.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at privacy@pitchpulse.com.
